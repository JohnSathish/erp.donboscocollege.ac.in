Welcome to Admission 2026–2027 Portal

Important Steps to Follow Before Registration

  1. 1 Register using a valid Email ID and Mobile Number.
  2. 2 After successful registration, login credentials will be sent to your registered email.
  3. 3 Log in and complete your Personal Details. Click "Save Details" after filling.
  4. 4 Enter Class X and XII marks, including aggregate percentage, in the Marks Section and click "Next/Update".

Note: Registration to the portal does NOT guarantee admission.

Important

  • Admission will be granted based on merit and the decision of the Admission Committee.
  • The decision of the Admission Committee will be final.
  • Ensure your Email ID and Mobile Number are correct and active throughout the admission process.
  • Do not miss important updates—check your email and portal regularly.
